# Allianz Trade

> Allianz Trade APIs enable businesses to automate trade credit insurance management including cover requests, credit limit monitoring, payment overdue reporting, claims management, and company credit grading. Built on secure REST architecture with OAuth2 (client credentials), the APIs support three business lines: Trade Credit Insurance, E-Commerce B2B, and Surety/Guarantee.

This file is generated by API Evangelist from the apis.yml index and the artifacts in this repository. It is not published by Allianz Trade.

## APIs

- [Allianz Trade Payment Overdues API](https://developers.allianz-trade.com/welcome-trade-credit-insurance-api-world/payment-overdues): Report payment overdues, request extension periods, and manage debt rescheduling. Overdue category codes: OVD (default), EXP (extension), RES (rescheduling). Base URL https://api.allianz-trade.com/v1
- [Allianz Trade Company Grade API](https://developers.allianz-trade.com/api-catalogue): Retrieve client creditworthiness grades after a cover request, with bulk grading support. Base URL https://api.allianz-trade.com/v1
- [Allianz Trade Claims API](https://developers.allianz-trade.com/api-catalogue): Access and manage insurance claims declared to Allianz Trade from within ERP systems. Base URL https://api.allianz-trade.com/v1
- [Allianz Trade Policy API](https://developers.allianz-trade.com/api-catalogue): Manage trade credit insurance policy portfolios, including joint insured policies. Base URL https://api.allianz-trade.com/v1
